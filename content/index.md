As it turns out, no matter how much I am talking in real life, the endless threads and loops and knots of words do not end inside my head. There is always something going on - a completely obscure scenario, the 253th case of "what if", the way I looked wrong at the direction of that strange person on the street, the absolute new habit I picked up on the way or how I would have behaved if I was *this* character from *that* book. 
And after exhausting all my human(-ear) resources, I have to turn back to my good ole' pen & paper. 

**So, I write.**
To give my brain a lax time. To have my words expressed. To liberate all those chains and emotion I find myself in.
